# AI Agent Risk Taxonomy

This document defines the risk taxonomy for the AIUC-1 SOC 2 Azure Foundry Compliance Lab project, in accordance with AIUC-1 control C001. It establishes a framework for classifying and prioritizing risks associated with the use of autonomous AI agents.

## Risk Severity Levels

Risks are categorized into four severity levels: Critical, High, Medium, and Low. The severity level is determined by the potential impact of the risk on the project, its objectives, and its stakeholders.

| Severity | Description |
|---|---|
| **Critical** | A risk that, if realized, would have a severe and immediate impact on the project's ability to meet its objectives. This could include a major security breach, significant data loss, or a complete failure of the AI agent system. |
| **High** | A risk that, if realized, would have a significant impact on the project. This could include a partial system failure, a minor security breach, or a significant degradation in the quality of the AI agents' outputs. |
| **Medium** | A risk that, if realized, would have a moderate impact on the project. This could include a temporary system outage, a minor data quality issue, or a noticeable but not critical degradation in the AI agents' performance. |
| **Low** | A risk that, if realized, would have a minimal impact on the project. This could include a minor user interface bug, a temporary performance degradation, or a cosmetic issue with the AI agents' outputs. |
